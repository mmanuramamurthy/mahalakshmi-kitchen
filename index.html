Chapter II

It begins
at dawn.

Long before the first customer arrives, our kitchen stirs to life. The batter — soaked, ground, and fermented overnight — is ready. The cast-iron tawa is seasoned with memory.

For generations, South Indian families have woken before sunrise to prepare dosa batter. This is not fast food. This is ritual. We honour that ritual every single day in our kitchen in Bangalore.

Chapter III

Only the finest
ingredients survive.

We reject shortcuts. We reject compromise. Every element of our batter and filling is chosen with obsessive care.

01 Hand-ground Spices

Sourced from Karnataka's spice markets. Ground fresh each morning, never pre-mixed.

02 Idli Rice

Short-grain rice chosen for its perfect starch content — the backbone of crispy batter.

03 Urad Dal

Split black lentils, soaked for hours. They give the dosa its signature lightness.

04 Farm Potatoes & Ghee

The masala filling uses potatoes from local farms. The ghee comes from trusted dairies — golden, clarified, real.

Chapter V

Six dosas.
Six characters.

Each one a distinct personality. Each one made to order, served with sambar and two fresh chutneys.

01

Masala Dosa

Our icon. A wafer-thin golden crepe folded around spiced potato masala, served with coconut chutney and sambar. The one that started it all.

⭐ Our Signature
02

Ghee Roast

For those who believe butter makes everything better. Slow-roasted in farm ghee until it shimmers gold. Crispy at the edge, rich at the soul.

✨ Chef's Pick
03

Mysore Masala

Bold. Defiant. A fiery Mysore red chutney spread inside before the masala is folded in. Not for the faint of heart — but impossible to forget.

🌶 For Spice Lovers
04

Rava Dosa

Lacy, delicate, unpredictable. Made with semolina and rice flour, each Rava Dosa is unique — its pattern of holes a fingerprint of the batter's mood.

🌼 Light & Crispy
05

Onion Dosa

Fresh white onions and green chillies pressed into the batter as it spreads. The sizzle tells you it's ready. The aroma tells you it's right.

🧅 Rustic & Bold
06

Plain Dosa

The purist's choice. Nothing hidden. Nothing added. Just batter, heat, and skill. If our Plain Dosa impresses you, you understand what we stand for.
